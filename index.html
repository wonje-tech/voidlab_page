Research Areas

Our lab focuses on building AI systems for next-generation autonomous mobility.

🚗

Autonomous Driving Systems

End-to-end intelligent systems for smart mobility — from perception to planning and decision-making in complex real-world environments.

👁️

2D & 3D Computer Vision

Deep learning-based scene understanding, object detection, tracking, and semantic segmentation for both camera and LiDAR modalities.

📡

Multi-modal Sensor Fusion

Combining camera, LiDAR, and radar data to build robust, weather-resilient perception pipelines for autonomous vehicles.

🗺️

HD Map & SLAM

High-definition map construction, lane-level road marking mapping, simultaneous localization and mapping for precise autonomous navigation.

🧠

Vision-Language Models

Leveraging VLM-based reasoning for enhanced autonomous driving map construction and pedestrian safety analysis.

🔧

Data Generation & Refinement

Synthetic data generation using diffusion models and dataset curation pipelines to accelerate training of autonomous driving models.
